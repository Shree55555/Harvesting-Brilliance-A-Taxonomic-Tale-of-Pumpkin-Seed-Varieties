Our AI-powered system simplifies this process by analyzing key morphological features such as area, perimeter, aspect ratio, roundness, and compactness with high precision.

Using a trained machine learning model, the platform detects hidden patterns within the data that are not easily visible to the human eye. This enables accurate classification of pumpkin seed varieties in real time, ensuring faster and more reliable results compared to traditional methods.
